Iron Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books
by Marvel Comics. The character was
co-created by writer
and editor Stan Lee, developed by scripter
Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby.
The character made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39
(cover dated March 1963), and received his own title in Iron Man #1 (May 1968). Also in 1963,
the character founded
the Avengers alongside Thor, Ant-Man, Wasp and the Hulk.
